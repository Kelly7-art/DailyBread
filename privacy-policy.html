Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2025
Welcome to Daily Bread. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app. Please read it carefully — it is short and straightforward.
📋 1. Information We Collect

Daily Bread is a simple Bible verse app. We do not collect, store or share any personal information about you.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address or phone number
  • Your location data
  • Your device identifiers
  • Any usage data or analytics
  • Any account or login information
🌐 2. Internet Access

Daily Bread uses an internet connection solely to fetch the daily Bible verse from the OurManna API (ourmanna.com). This request does not include any personal data from your device.

No other network requests are made by the app.

❤️ 3. Saved Favourites

Verses you save to your Favourites are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your phone and is never transmitted to any server.

📤 4. Sharing

When you use the Share feature, you share content directly through your device's built-in sharing system (e.g. WhatsApp, SMS, Facebook). Daily Bread does not have access to what you share or who you share it with.

👶 5. Children's Privacy

Daily Bread does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for all ages.

🔒 6. Third-Party Services

Daily Bread uses the following third-party service:

  • OurManna API — used to retrieve the daily Bible verse. Please refer to OurManna's own privacy policy at ourmanna.com for details on their data practices.

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics tools or tracking services.

✏️ 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
